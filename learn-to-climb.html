Learn to climb at Fjellhallen

We offer comprehensive certification courses throughout the year, providing new climbers with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the vertical terrain confidently. Whether you're a beginner eager to grasp the fundamentals or someone looking to refine their techniques, our expert instructors are committed to guiding you every step of the way. Our courses cover essential aspects such as safety protocols, proper equipment usage, and climbing techniques suitable for various levels. Feel free to contact us about when the next course will be held.

Tips and tricks before taking your climbing test

  • Don't hesitate to clarify any doubts or ask questions about the test format, rules, or any concerns you may have.
  • Ensure you have a solid understanding of safety procedures, including proper harness and helmet usage, knot tying, and communication signals.
  • Know the specific requirements and criteria for the climbing test.
  • Develop a positive and focused mindset. Stay calm and composed during the test.
