Our facilities

Whether you're taking your first steps into the exciting world of climbing or you're a seasoned climber seeking new challenges, Fjellhallen climbing wall has something for everyone. Our facility boasts a diverse array of wall types designed to cater to climbers of all skill levels.

Read more about our facilities

Our community

Fjellhallen climbing wall has a great community of climbers with all types of backgrounds and skill levels. Both the local climbing club and the student climbing club offers fun trips and other events throughout the year.

Read more about our community

Interested in learning how to climb?

We offer climbing courses to anyone who wants to enjoy the sport of climbing. Our instructors will teach you everything you need to know about ropes, gear, and climbing technique. Everything you need to start your climbing journey.

Read more about our courses

Passes and Rental gear

We provide passes and gear rental choices to both students and regular users. Students get special prices for passes. Furthermore, our range of rental gear guarantees that climbers, irrespective of their experience level, can access top-notch equipment for an optimal climbing experience.
